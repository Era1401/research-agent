# SearchNext
An Agentic AI Research Assistant that serves as an end-to-end intelligent research companion for students, researchers, and professionals. 


### Overview

Research Aid is an intelligent research companion designed to simplify academic and technical research. Instead of simply searching for papers, it autonomously retrieves, analyzes, synthesizes, and visualizes knowledge from multiple research sources.

The platform helps researchers, students, and professionals spend less time searching and more time discovering meaningful insights.

### Problem Statement

Researchers often spend hours searching through journals, papers, preprints, and technical articles scattered across multiple platforms.

Research Aid addresses this challenge by:

- Retrieving relevant research from multiple academic sources
- Generating concise literature reviews
- Detecting research gaps and citation opportunities
- Identifying emerging research trends
- Visualizing research knowledge through interactive dashboards

### Features
- AI-powered literature review generation
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Multi-agent architecture
- Research paper summarization
- Citation analysis
- Trend prediction
- Knowledge graph visualization
- Topic clustering
- Research dashboard
- IBM watsonx Orchestrate chat assistant
- Multimodal document support (PDFs, notes, images)
